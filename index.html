What

Homestuck is a webcomic created by Andrew Hussie, it started in 2009 and concluded in 2016. The series centers on a group of teenagers who enter in an adventure through the installation of a beta copy of the upcoming computer game "Sburb".
The comic consists of a combination of static images, animated GIFs and message logs, as well as animations and games made with Adobe Flash. It has been noted for its complex plot and considerable length; over 8,000 pages and 800,000 words.
